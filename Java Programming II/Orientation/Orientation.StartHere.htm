Orientation, Start Here

All students enrolled in this course are required to complete online orientation at the beginning of the course.

Open each of the links in the Orientation module in your browser. Read the material exposed by those links and complete any instructions that you find there.

Then select Orientation Test00. You must successfully complete Test00 to meet the attendance requirement explained elsewhere.

If you have any questions regarding this orientation procedure, send an email to your instructor with your questions.
