Overall Grade or Current Grade

When you select Gradebook in the navigation bar, you may see something titled Overall Grade or Current Grade in the upper-right portion of the page. Please be aware that this is not your official grade in the course. It is simply an estimate at a given point in time as to what your final grade might be if you keep up the same quality of work for the remainder of the course.
