Self Introduction

The Discussions feature makes it possible for students to engage in discussions with other students and to disclose whatever information they wish to disclose. There is an Anonymous Forum that allows students to engage in discussions without identifying themselves. There is also an Open Forum that allows students to identify themselves and engage in discussions with other students.

If you choose to do so, you may introduce yourself to the rest of the class and tell us a little bit about yourself by selecting Discussions in the navigation bar. This is not a requirement of the course.

I don't normally monitor the discussions, so don't make a post there and expect me to respond. You need to contact me directly if you want to discuss something with me.

Prof. Baldwin
