Policy Regarding Late Work

Late assignments:

If you are unable to earn credit for an assignment by its due date, you are still eligible to earn credit for the assignment. You can submit a correct solution for the assignment by 11:59 PM on the due date and time for the last assignment and receive 80-percent credit.

The determination as to what is late and what is not late will depend entirely on Blackboard. If Blackboard flags an assignment as LATE, it will be subject to a 20% late penalty regardless of how late it was submitted. Since your clock may not be synchronized with the clock in Blackboard, it would be wise for you to always submit your assignments at least a few minutes early according to your clock.

Excessive missing assignments

If you accumulate three or more missing assignments - meaning that neither an on-time nor a late submission was made for each of the missing assignments - you may be dropped from the course unless there is a very good reason for your failure to submit the assignments. In other words you may not put off submitting large numbers of assignments until the end of the semester expecting to receive credit for those assignments.

Late quizzes and tests

Quizzes and tests must be completed by their due dates in order to earn credit.
